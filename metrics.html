Role of Feedback in Model Monitoring

Positive and negative user feedback provides a post-deployment monitoring mechanism. Negative feedback helps identify incorrect classifications, missed detections, and other prediction issues, while positive feedback provides a useful signal of model performance and prediction reliability in real-world use.
